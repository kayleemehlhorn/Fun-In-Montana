The City of Whitefish, MT
AKA: Stumptown

Now mainly known for its ski resort and lake life (among many other activities), Whitefish was established in 1905 after the infamous Great Northern Railway found its home here.

The town is located on the western side of the continental divide, near Glacier National Park.

As of 2023, Whitefish has a population on 9,943 and is growing at a rate of 8.21%. The average income per household in Whitefish is $111,754, making it quite a desireable thus expensive place to live.

Living in Whitefish offers a sparse suburban feel, and is one of the best places to live in Montana
