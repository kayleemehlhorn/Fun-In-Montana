About Helena

Establised in 1864

Helena was founded as a gold camp during the Montana gold rush. At the time, Helena became a wealthy city where about 50 millionaires once lived. The wealth in the area contributed to the city's prominent Victorian architecture you may see as you explore the city.

Helena is situated in the western part of the state, surrounded by the Rocky Mountains. It is a city that is generally considered a small urban area. While it is the capital city of the state, it has a smaller population compared to larger urban centers.

In 2021, Helena had a population of 82.4k people with a median age oof 42.1 and a median houseold income of $66,862.
